JoshMoney

Features

iOS & Android

Offline-first

Core bookkeeping works without an account. Data stays on your device by default.

Receipts & expenses

Quick entry, receipt scanning, merchant learning, and ITC support.

Canadian invoices

BN/HST, PDF share, partial payments, and email delivery.

Payroll reference

Bi-weekly, weekly, or monthly payslips with CPP/EI/tax estimates (reference only).

Mileage log

CRA kilometre rates for deductible business travel you enter manually.

Tax exports

T2125 and GST/HST summaries for Wealthsimple Tax or your CPA.

Optional cloud sync

Apple or Google sign-in for optional Supabase sync; receipts can backup to iCloud or Drive.

Team (optional)

Invite teammates by email; shared books with per-person entries when cloud sync is enabled.

Not tax advice

Reports and payslip deductions are for reference — confirm with your accountant before remitting or filing.

No money movement

No payments, bank transfers, loans, or CRA NETFILE submission through the app.
